Tortilla de patatas

Description

Tortilla de patatas, also known as Spanish omelette, is a traditional Spanish dish made with eggs, potatoes, and onions. It's a simple yet delicious meal that can be served warm or at room temperature.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 6 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
  • Crusty bread for serving (optional)
  • Chorizo or bell peppers for added flavor (optional)
  • Cheese for a cheesy twist (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced potatoes and onion, and cook until the potatoes are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Drain the potatoes and onion from the oil and add them to the bowl with the eggs. Mix well.
  5. Wipe the skillet clean and add a little more olive oil. Pour in the potato-egg mixture.
  6. Cook on low heat for about 5-7 minutes until the bottom is set.
  7. Carefully flip the tortilla using a plate and cook the other side for another 5-7 minutes.
  8. Slide the tortilla onto a serving plate and let it cool slightly before slicing.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature with crusty bread on the side.
  11. Enjoy your delicious tortilla de patatas!

Optional Recommendations

  • Optional: Add chorizo or bell peppers for extra flavor.
  • Optional: Serve with a side of aioli or garlic mayonnaise.
  • Optional: Use sweet potatoes for a different twist.
  • Optional: Add spinach or kale for a healthier version.
  • Optional: Serve with a side salad for a complete meal.
  • Optional: Use a non-stick skillet for easier flipping.
  • Optional: Add a pinch of smoked paprika for a smoky flavor.
  • Optional: Serve with a glass of red wine for a traditional Spanish experience.
  • Optional: Use leftover tortilla in a sandwich or wrap.
  • Optional: Add diced tomatoes for freshness.
  • Optional: Serve with olives or pickles on the side.
  • Optional: Use a mix of different cheeses for added flavor.
  • Optional: Add herbs like thyme or rosemary for extra aroma.
  • Optional: Serve with a side of roasted vegetables.
  • Optional: Add cheese for a cheesy twist.
