New York Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter until combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form the crust.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  5. Add 1 cup sugar and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.
  6. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  7. Add sour cream, flour, and salt, and mix until smooth.
  8. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is set and slightly jiggly.
  10. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for 1 hour to cool gradually.
  11. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  12. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
  13. Serve chilled, topped with fresh fruit or whipped cream if desired.
  14. Enjoy your delicious New York cheesecake!

Optional Recommendations

  • Optional: Add a fruit topping like strawberries or blueberries for extra flavor.
  • Optional: Drizzle with chocolate sauce for a decadent touch.
  • Optional: Use a water bath while baking to prevent cracks in the cheesecake.
  • Optional: Add a hint of lemon zest for a refreshing twist.
  • Optional: Serve with a side of whipped cream for added richness.
  • Optional: Use a food processor to blend the crust ingredients for a finer texture.
  • Optional: Experiment with different flavored cream cheeses for a unique taste.
  • Optional: Add a pinch of cinnamon to the crust for extra warmth.
  • Optional: Use a combination of graham crackers and chocolate cookies for the crust.
  • Optional: Top with caramel sauce for a sweet finish.
  • Optional: Garnish with mint leaves for a pop of color.
  • Optional: Serve with a side of coffee or tea for a perfect pairing.
